## Key Decisions

1. **No auto-sync** — Pat needs manual sync because he updates Halaxy immediately before creating reports
2. **Report type defaults to Initial** — it comes first in the clinical workflow
3. **No patient switching in editor** — Pat works on one report at a time, switching would be confusing
4. **Scores table format** — designed for DOCX/PDF export compatibility (simple table borders, no fancy CSS)
5. **State lifted to App level** — ensures report type and patient selection persist across all screens
